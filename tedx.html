Tedx

TEDx is a global platform that enables independent organizers to host local, self-organized events under the TED (Technology, Entertainment, Design) banner. These events feature a diverse range of speakers who share innovative ideas and thought-provoking insights in engaging talks, typically limited to 18 minutes. TEDx events span a wide array of topics, from science and technology to arts, culture, and social issues. They foster a sense of community and intellectual curiosity, inspiring attendees to think critically and creatively while sparking discussions that can lead to positive change. TEDx serves as a powerful catalyst for spreading ideas and facilitating meaningful conversations worldwide. We have been fortunate enough to open one such chapter in UVCE which withholds all the core values that TEDx stands for.
