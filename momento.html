MOMENTO

The official dance club of the college. This club is a lively place for students who love to dance. It's where dancers of all styles, like hip-hop and Bharatanatyam, can express themselves. The club's main goals are to encourage physical fitness, artistic expression, and an understanding of different cultures through dance. Members often join dance workshops, create their own dance routines, and perform at college events. It's not just about becoming better dancers; it's also about working together, boosting self-confidence, and feeling like part of a community. This club is a great way for students to relax, stay active, and enjoy the fun of dancing, making their college experience even more exciting.
