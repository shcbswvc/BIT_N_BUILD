KUTUHALA

It is the official quizzing club of the college. The logo stands for ‘Infinite Curiosity’. "KUTUHALA," is all about sparking curiosity and learning. They organize fun quiz competitions where students can test their knowledge and problem-solving skills. These quizzes cover a wide range of topics, from history to pop culture. The club also holds practice sessions and workshops to help members get better at quizzing. It's not just about being smart; it's also about working together and having fun while learning. KUTUHALA hosts different quizzes on various subjects that everyone can enjoy, making it an important and intellectually stimulating part of college life.
