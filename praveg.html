Praveg

Founded in January 2023, this is a student club led by students from across the branches. It is an attempt to bring back UVCE’s flagship motor vehicle manufacturing club SAE INDIA which has brought laurels to our university. The team at PRAVEG UVCE is driven by their common passion for building cars and racing in national events. Here, a healthy work environment is fostered thereby propelling their efforts towards achieving their goals. The team’s motto is: Dedication, Patience and Perseverance. The Logo stands for ‘Paving the way for racing excellence!’.
