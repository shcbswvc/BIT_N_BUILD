G2C2

The "Go Green, Go Clean" Club at the college is all about taking care of the environment and making our campus and community cleaner and more eco-friendly. Its main goals are to teach people to be more environmentally conscious, use sustainable practices, and educate about nature. Members do things like planting trees, recycling, reducing waste, and cleaning up the community. The club also holds events and workshops on topics like renewable energy and protecting the environment. But it's not just about doing things; it's about getting everyone to care for our planet. This club is like a spark for making positive changes to create a more eco-friendly future at the college and beyond.
