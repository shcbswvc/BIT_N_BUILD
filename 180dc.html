180DC

180 Degrees Consulting (180DC) is a global nonprofit organization that empowers university students to use their skills for positive social impact. Founded in 2007, 180DC has expanded to numerous chapters across the world. UVCE being the only one to open one in Bengaluru. Its mission is to provide pro bono consulting services to nonprofit organizations and social enterprises, helping them address challenges and maximize their impact. Student volunteers, often studying diverse fields, collaborate to develop practical solutions and strategies for their clients. Through this hands-on experience, 180DC not only benefits the social sector but also equips future leaders with valuable skills and a passion for social change. It exemplifies the power of youth-driven social entrepreneurship and has catalyzed positive change in countless communities.
