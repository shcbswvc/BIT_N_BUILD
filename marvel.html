MARVEL

In short for Makerspace for Advanced Research Vital Education and Learning. Marvel at UVCE is poised to spur genuine passion in every learner, inspire decisive action and redefine conventional education. The goal of this club is to turn UVCE into a hub of research and innovation. There are three tracks that a student can choose from, which are the student track, project track and the competitive track. While the admission to Marvel’s student track is limited, students are invites to continue upskilling themselves through the Open learner programme. One can access any of Marvel’s courses, utilize their own resources and complete the tasks at their own pace. Upon completion of the coursework and submission of the final report, the student will receive a certificate equivalent of the official Marvel batch that highlights their achievement and dedication to continuous learning.
